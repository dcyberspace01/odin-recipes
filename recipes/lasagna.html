Easy Lasagna Recipe

Brief Description

Here is a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade easy lasagna: Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
